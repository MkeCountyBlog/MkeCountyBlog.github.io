Using Atom/RSS Feeds

Atom feeds, earlier known as RSS (Really Simple Syndication), allow you to follow websites without visiting. For example, if you enjoy Milwaukee County Blog but don't want to visit all of the time to figure out when new posts are up, you can subscribe to the Atom feed and it will show up in your app. So how do you do that? Look below!

  1. Find a Feed Reader! Below are just a few options.
    1. FreshRSS - Android/iOS/Web Browser
    2. Fiery Feeds - iOS
    3. Feed Bin - Android/iOS/Web Browser
    4. Feeder - Android
    5. Lots Of Others
  2. Click or copy the Atom/Feed/RSS link at the top of this page.
  3. Open your selected Feed Reader from step #1.
  4. Click add feed (or equivalent.)
  5. Save and you are now following MCB!

You can do the same thing with lots of other websites to follow all of your blogs and news sites in one place. I personally use and love Feed Bin (#3 above.)

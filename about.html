About

The rough purpose of this blog is to provide a place to organize my thoughts and, as a byproduct, open them up to discussion with a local audience. Having lived my entire life within this wonderful county, many of the topics will be pulled from local headlines. Occasionally I will write about technology mostly in relation to free and open-source software.

Milwaukee County Blog is built with the fantastic FOSS static site generator Publii. It is hosted (for free!) with Github Pages. MCB's logos are manipulations of images originally from MCTS by way of a WUWM article. Krita was used to make some adjustments to the originals and personalize them.

That's enough attributions. Thanks for taking a look and feel free to join in!

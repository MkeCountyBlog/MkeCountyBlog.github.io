Looking Back At Concordia College

Colin White

A short audio story exploring life on campus and why the college moved to Mequon. It features former Concordia College student Wayne Braun; presently the senior pastor at Fishers of Men Lutheran Church in Texas.  He and his wife were members of the first class to receive a four year bachelor of arts degree from the college in 1980.

Concordia to Wgema: 40 Years of Transitions

Colin White

The Concordia neighborhood in Milwaukee's West End was named after Concordia College which was located there from 1881 until they relocated to Mequon in 1983. The campus and many surrounding residences were sold to the Forest County Potawatomi in 1990. The following stories provide an overview of how the campus has changed over time.

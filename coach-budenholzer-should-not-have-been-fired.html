Coach Budenholzer Should Not Have Been Fired

Colin White

From a Casino.org post.

Budenholzer led the Bucks to a Central Division crown in five consecutive seasons. He posted a 271-120 record, and won over 69% of his regular-season games during his five-year stint in Milwaukee.

A highly successful coach that guided the Bucks to their first championship in 50 years should be revered not canned. The Bucks are dumping a top 5 coach.
